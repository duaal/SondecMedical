about us

Sondos Medical was established in the golden age of the Saudi national industry, and Sondos Medical Company along with its partners was able to excel in the national industry by investing in talented Saudi youth and national resources to create excellence and quality for our national products

Our vision

We look forward to a gigantic National industry worthy of the size of the kingdom and compete with our qualities with the finest industries in the world with the goal to export to the world under the banner of Made in Saudi Arabia

Our vlaues

Development and innovation
We attract distinctive professional competencies and share creative ideas with them, and help them develop through programs to create unique and strong ideas with high efficiency.

Transparency and clarity
We follow professional work standards that guarantee Transparency in all stages of our work and help create a positive competition to provide a special productive environment at work

Professional
We embed in our team and along with success partners moral values - fairness and integrity - belonging and loyalty - honesty and respect - cooperation and participation - flexibility and innovation.

Teamwork
We work together to create a strong future national young leaders who merge into work according to a constructive approach that returns to our company and our partners with a real added value .

Mastery and sincerity
We believe that achieving success lies in the satisfaction of our success partners, including customers, employees and suppliers. Therefore, we believe that sincerity and perfection in work is the only and shortest path to reach our goal.
